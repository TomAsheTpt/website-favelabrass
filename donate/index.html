What your gift does

Everything we teach is free for the students. Donations pay for teachers, instruments and the day-to-day running of the programme.

Music lessons in public schools

Weekly brass and percussion classes inside five partner public schools in Rio, for children who would otherwise never touch an instrument.

An advanced course in the favela

Five sessions a week in Pereira da Silva, the community where the project began: individual lessons, music theory and band rehearsals.

Instruments to take home

Students borrow trumpets, trombones, saxophones and drums to practise at home, and perform across the city all year round.
