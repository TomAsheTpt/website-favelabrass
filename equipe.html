Lideranca Leadership

Tom Ashe

Diretor Executivo e Fundador Executive Director and Founder

Trompetista britanico radicado no Rio de Janeiro desde 2010. Fundou a Favela Brass em 2014 com a visao de levar educacao musical de qualidade para comunidades carentes. British trumpet player based in Rio de Janeiro since 2010. Founded Favela Brass in 2014 with the vision of bringing quality music education to underserved communities.

Wesley de Araujo

Diretor Musical Music Director

Trombonista e tubista formado pela comunidade Pereira da Silva. Ex-aluno do projeto, hoje lidera o programa musical e rege as bandas avancadas. Trombonist and tuba player from the Pereira da Silva community. A former student of the project, he now leads the music programme and conducts the advanced bands.
